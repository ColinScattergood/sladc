Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02/02/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Scattered Labs LLC and TicketDraft. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, "Services").

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Create an account or register for our Services
  • Contact us for support or inquiries
  • Participate in surveys, promotions, or other interactive features
  • Submit content or communicate with us

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, payment details, usernames, passwords, and any other information you choose to provide.

b. Information We Automatically Collect

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device model, device identifiers
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on our Services, clicks, navigation paths, access times
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Data collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tools
  • Location Information: If you enable location services

c. Information from Third Parties

We may collect information from third-party sources, such as social media platforms, business partners, or analytics providers like Google Analytics and Pendo. These services may collect and process data such as:

  • App usage data (e.g., screen views, interactions, session duration)
  • Device and browser information
  • IP addresses (which may be anonymized)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our Services
  • Understand and analyze how you use our Services
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionalities
  • Communicate with you, including for customer service, updates, marketing, and promotional purposes
  • Process transactions and manage orders
  • Send text messages and push notifications
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: Third parties assisting in delivering our Services
  • Business Partners: In connection with co-branded offerings or promotions
  • Analytics Providers: Non-personal, aggregated, or anonymized data with services like Google Analytics and Pendo
  • Business Transfers: During negotiations of mergers, sales, acquisitions, or similar events
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with legal requirements or protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: As directed by you

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Security of Your Information

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures to safeguard your personal information. While we strive to protect your data, no system is completely secure. We encourage you to protect your information by using strong passwords and keeping them confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to or correction of your personal information
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Manage preferences for receiving marketing communications
  • Delete Data: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@scatteredlabsapps.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us at privacy@scatteredlabsapps.com to have it removed.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

10. International Users

If you access our Services from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective when posted with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
